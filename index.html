Privacy Policy - Study Pomodoro

Last updated: 2026-06-23

Study Pomodoro is a focus timer app published by "By Carrola". This policy explains what data the app handles and how. It is written to be simple and honest.

Using the app without an account

By default, Study Pomodoro works fully offline and does not require an account. When you use the app without signing in, all your data (focus statistics, timer settings, language, preferences) is stored only on your own device. Nothing is sent to us or to any server.

Optional Google sign-in and sync

You can choose to sign in with your Google account to sync your progress across your devices. This is entirely optional. If you sign in:

  • We receive your Google account identifier and email address, used only to identify your account and link your synced data to you.
  • Your focus statistics and app settings are stored in the cloud so they can be synced between your devices.

We never see or store your Google password. Sign-in is handled by Google.

Where data is stored

Synced data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore), operated by Google. Access is restricted so that each user can only read and write their own data.

How we use your data

Your data is used only to provide the sync feature - keeping your focus stats and settings consistent across your devices. We do not use it for anything else.

What we do NOT do

  • No advertising.
  • No third-party advertising or analytics trackers.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Deleting your data

  • You can sign out at any time; your data stays on your device.
  • To delete your synced account data, contact us at the address below and we will remove it.

Children

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

This policy may be updated; the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.
